I am an assistant professor of mathematics at Bryn Mawr College. I received my PhD in 2017 from Tulane University. My research focuses on commutative algebra, combinatorics, and geometry.

I am one of the co-founders of Meet a Mathematician!, a collection of short video interviews with mathematicians.

I am a Project NExT Gold'21 Fellow.

Before Bryn Mawr:

  • 2022-2023: Science Research Initiative Fellow, College of Science, University of Utah
  • 2021-2022: Research Associate, Department of Mathematics, University of Utah
  • 2017-2021: Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics and Statics, University of South Alabama
